Preventative Measures that will be taken For Covid-19

During GSLMUN 2021

-Verification of HES codes and fever check at the entrance. The delegate being refused the right to participate in GSLMUN 2021 in case of the detection of fever levels above 37.5 degrees or if the delegate is clasified as “risky” in the HES app.

-The sitting arrangement being designed specifically to leave 1.5 meters and/or 1 empty chair between the delegates

-The disposition of free disenfectants and face masks in the conference rooms and corridors for the usage of Participants.

-Abiding by all the protocols set by the ministries of Health and Education.

-The obligation that all participants must have had 2 doses of the vaccine or have had been tested for coronavirus within 48 hours.

-Obligatory facemask usage during the duration of the event.
